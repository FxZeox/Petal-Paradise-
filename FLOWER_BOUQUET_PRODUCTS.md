1. Go to **Admin Dashboard** (`/admin`)
2. Click **"+ Add New Bracelet"** button (this actually works for any product type)
3. Fill in the product details
4. Click **"Add Product"**

## Tips for Adding Flower Products

✅ **Product Images:**
- Use high-quality images (at least 600x600px)
- Show flowers in a nice vase or wrapping
- Ensure good lighting
- Take clear, professional-looking photos

✅ **Descriptions:**
- Keep descriptions under 12 words (as per system limit)
- Mention key flowers and colors
- Example: "Red roses with green foliage and baby's breath"

✅ **Pricing:**
- Consider flower quality, variety, and arrangement quality
- Include delivery and wrapping costs
- Keep prices competitive

✅ **Customization:**
- You can modify the product name, price, and description anytime
- Customers can request custom arrangements via WhatsApp

## Product Management

### To Edit a Product:
1. Go to Admin Dashboard
2. Find the product in the table
3. Click **"Edit"** button
4. Update the information
5. Click **"Update Product"**

### To Delete a Product:
1. Go to Admin Dashboard
2. Find the product in the table
3. Click **"Delete"** button
4. Confirm deletion
